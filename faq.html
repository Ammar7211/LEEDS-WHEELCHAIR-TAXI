Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wheelchair-accessible taxi services in Leeds.

Do you provide wheelchair-accessible taxi in Leeds?

Yes. We provide wheelchair-accessible taxi services across Leeds and surrounding areas. Our vehicles are properly equipped to safely accommodate wheelchairs, and our drivers are experienced in assisted travel.

How do I book a wheelchair taxi in Leeds?

You can book a wheelchair-accessible taxi in Leeds by calling us directly or using our online booking form. We recommend booking in advance where possible, especially for medical appointments or airport transfers.

Are your drivers trained to assist wheelchair users?

Yes. All drivers we work with understand how to assist wheelchair users safely and respectfully. Assistance is provided calmly and patiently, ensuring every passenger feels comfortable and supported.

What types of journeys do you cover?

We provide wheelchair taxi services for:
  • Medical and hospital appointments
  • Airport transfers
  • Long-distance travel
  • Care home visits
  • Everyday local journeys

Do you operate 24/7?

Yes. Leeds Wheelchair taxi operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including evenings, weekends, and bank holidays.

Can you accommodate different types of wheelchairs?

We can accommodate most standard wheelchairs. If you have a larger or powered wheelchair, please let us know at the time of booking so we can ensure the correct vehicle is provided.

How much does a wheelchair-accessible taxi cost?

Prices vary depending on distance, journey type, and time of travel. We offer clear and fair pricing and are happy to provide a quote before your journey via our calculator or phone.

Are your taxi suitable for elderly passengers?

Yes. Our service is ideal for elderly passengers and those with limited mobility. Drivers provide respectful assistance and take extra care to ensure a smooth and comfortable journey.

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes. We offer wheelchair-accessible airport transfers from Leeds to all major UK airports. This includes assistance with loading, travel, and drop-off.

Which areas do you cover?

We cover Leeds and surrounding areas, including: Leeds City Centre, Headingley, Roundhay, Chapel Allerton, Beeston, Pudsey, and nearby locations.

Why choose Leeds Wheelchair taxi?

We focus on:
  • Reliable wheelchair-accessible vehicles
  • Professional, caring drivers
  • 24/7 availability
  • Respect, dignity, and comfort
