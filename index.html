Why I Started Leeds Wheelchair Taxis

I started Leeds Wheelchair Taxis after seeing first-hand how difficult it can be for wheelchair users to find a reliable wheelchair-accessible taxi in Leeds. Too often, people struggled to book suitable transport, faced long waiting times, or were collected in vehicles that were not fully accessible.

Many drivers simply weren’t trained to assist passengers with mobility needs. What should have been a straightforward taxi journey became stressful and undignified — and I knew there was a clear need for a better wheelchair taxi service in Leeds.

My goal was to create a service where accessibility comes first, not as an extra, but as the standard. That’s why I partnered with professional drivers who share the same values — patience, understanding, and respectful customer care.

Together, we provide properly equipped wheelchair-accessible taxi across Leeds and surrounding areas, offering safe, comfortable, and dependable transport for medical appointments, hospital visits, airport transfers, and everyday travel — available 24/7.

This business is about more than transport. It’s about independence, dignity, and peace of mind for wheelchair users and their families.
